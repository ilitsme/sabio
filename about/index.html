About us

Meet SabioTrade

We are a proprietary trading firm equipping talented traders with the financial backing needed to succeed in the global financial markets. Operating on a profit-sharing model, we empower traders to retain up to 90% of their profits while assuming all associated risks. 

Our history

2022

2022

Pioneering Amidst Change & Seeking Solutions

Shortly after the surge in online income queries during the 2020 pandemic, we uncovered a symbiotic business model benefiting both traders and the company. This led to the inception of SabioTrade, conceived as a playground for talented individuals with limited capital.

Throughout the year, the SabioTrade team has been involved in strategic planning and development. We've been crafting unique solutions, creating an adapted prop trading ecosystem, and searching for the best offers.

July - November 2023

July - November 2023

The Big Launch

In July 2023, SabioTrade officially launched its operations, unveiling a distinctive traderoom combined with the dashboard — a market-unique solution allowing traders to perform all operations within a unified interface.

During this period, we introduced several enhancements, including crypto payments, localization in six languages, real-time support across four channels, an educational section, a Telegram educational channel, and a vibrant Discord community.

December 2023

December 2023

Accelerated Ascent

SabioTrade swiftly made significant strides, earning recognition from clients, partners, and the media within a short timeframe. Our commitment to attentive listening to traders allows us to fine-tune our product in real-time and deliver one of the best prop trading solutions in the industry.

2024

2024

Grand Plans

SabioTrade has some significant updates planned for 2024. Firstly, the improved SabioTraderoom: added the ability to trade crypto assets, re-designed the asset selector, and optimized the panels for a higher convenience.

The SabioDashboard has also become more functional: we streamlined the payment and verification processes, introduced new visual indicators reflecting clients' performance and progress in assessments, and completely redesigned the visual appearance.

On top of that, we're introducing a new mobile app — a feature that sets us apart from most proprietary trading firms. Our application adheres to the high standards of the mobile market, allowing users to access SabioTraderoom on the go from their mobile devices, receive signals, and more.

Finally, we're thrilled to invite all traders to the Sabio Boot Camp, an ecosystem platform designed for trader training. Here, we feature exclusive educational materials, Q&A sessions, and live trading sessions with expert investors, among other offerings.

Mission statement

At SabioTrade, we aim to create a risk-free and affordable trading ecosystem for skilled traders by providing funding, cutting-edge technology, and community support to help people achieve ambitious financial goals.

Vision statement

SabioTrade envisions becoming the ultimate hub for traders, merging innovative trading solutions, top-tier education, and a supportive community in a seamless ecosystem. Our commitment lies in funding qualified traders, empowering them to attain unparalleled success in the financial markets.
